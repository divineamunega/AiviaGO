FAQs

Fly into our FAQ section get informed

How do I book a flight?
You can easily book a flight on our website by selecting your desired departure and arrival cities, dates, and number of passengers. Once you have selected your flight, you can enter your personal and payment information to complete the booking process.
What is the baggage allowance for my flight?
The baggage allowance varies depending on the airline and fare class you have selected. You can find the baggage allowance for your specific flight on the airline's website or by contacting their customer service.
Can I change or caancel my flight
Yes, you may be able to change or cancel your flight depending on the fare class and airline you have selected. Some tickets may be non-refundable, while others may be eligible for a fee or credit towards a future flight. You can check the fare rules and contact our customer service to make changes or cancellations.
How do I check-in for my flight?
You can check-in for your flight on the airline's website or mobile app, or at the airport check-in counter. Most airlines offer online check-in up to 24 hours before departure.
What documents do I need to bring to the airport?
You will need to bring a valid passport or government-issued ID and any necessary visas or travel documents required for your destination. It's also a good idea to bring a printed or digital copy of your flight itinerary.
What if my flight is delayed or cancelled?
If your flight is delayed or cancelled, the airline will typically notify you and provide options for rebooking or compensation. You can also check the status of your flight on the airline's website or by contacting their customer service.
